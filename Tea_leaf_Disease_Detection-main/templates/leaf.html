OUR PROJECT ON TEA LEAVES DISEASE

Tea leaves pictures can be used for the prediction of various tea leaves diseases which causes huge loss in the agricultural system every year. The proposed system, Tea Leaves Image Processing System, helps in the analysis of leaves and thereby helps in predicting various leaf diseases. The input to the proposed system is the Tea Leaves Image. The leaves portion is segmented and leaves color, shape, and texture features are extracted and taken together to form a feature vector, and then the analysis of leaves is done, which is used for the diagnosis of various tea leaves diseases. This proposed system will help farmers in the early diagnosis of diseases.
